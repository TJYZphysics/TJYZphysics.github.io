QUESTIONS

01OPTICS · VISION

自拟题：针孔太阳镜

Invent yourself: Pinhole sunglasses

Investigate the optimal arrangement of pinholes in an opaque film to produce sunglasses that reduce light intensity and help correct short-sightedness.

研究不透明薄膜中小孔的最佳排列方式，以制作出既能降低光照强度又能帮助纠正近视的太阳镜。

研究线索：

孔径、孔距、排列方式、眼球距离、屈光度与衍射极限

02DIFFRACTION · INSTRUMENT

光盘光谱仪

Disc spectrometer

Data tracks on optical media (e.g. CDs / Blu-ray discs) behave as high-quality reflection-diffraction gratings. When illuminated, these discs can disperse light into its spectral components. Construct a spectrometer using a digital optical disc. Optimise the design to maximise the resolving power and minimise optical aberrations.

光学介质（如 CD 或蓝光光盘）上的数据轨道可作为高质量反射衍射光栅，受光照时能将光分解为光谱成分。使用数字光盘制作光谱仪，并优化设计，以最大化分辨本领并减少光学像差。

研究线索：

轨道间距、入射角、狭缝宽度、谱级、分辨本领与像差

03ROTATION · VISCOUS FLOW

蜗牛球

Snail ball

A hollow sphere contains a heavy ball and some viscous fluid. The rolling motion of such a sphere down an inclined plane is uneven and surprisingly slow. Explain and investigate this effect.

一个空心球内装有重球和一些黏性流体。这样的球沿斜面向下滚动时，运动不均匀且慢得惊人。解释并研究这一效应。

研究线索：

黏度、填充率、质量比、斜面倾角、滑移与能量耗散

04ELECTRICITY · ACOUSTICS

会歌唱的电容器

Singing capacitor

Certain types of ceramic capacitors emit an audible sound when an alternating voltage is applied across them. Investigate the characteristics of the sound produced and how they depend on relevant parameters, such as voltage, frequency, and capacitor properties.

当交流电压加在某些类型的陶瓷电容器两端时，它们会发出可听见的声音。研究所产生声音的特征，以及这些特征如何依赖于电压、频率和电容器性质等相关参数。

研究线索：

电压、频率、介电材料、几何尺寸、边界固定与谐振峰

05OSCILLATION · GEOMETRY

Y 形摆

Y-shaped pendulum

Construct a Bowditch pendulum (a pendulum whose strings form a "Y" shape). Investigate how the Lissajous patterns generated by the bob depend on the relevant parameters.

制作一个 Bowditch 摆（悬线呈 Y 形的摆）。研究摆球产生的李萨如图形如何依赖于相关参数。

研究线索：

分叉点位置、绳长比、初始条件、频率比、相位差与阻尼

06MEMBRANE · LASER

看见声音

Seeing sound

A thin rubber membrane is stretched over a circular frame and driven into vibration by a loudspeaker. A small mirror is attached to the membrane, and a laser beam is reflected off it onto a distant screen. Under certain conditions, the laser spot traces out complex, steady, or evolving geometric patterns. Investigate the relationship between the driving parameters, the membrane’s properties, and the resulting light patterns.

一张薄橡胶膜绷紧在圆形框架上，并由扬声器驱动振动。膜上附着一面小镜子，激光束经它反射到远处屏幕上。在特定条件下，激光点会描绘出复杂的、稳定的或不断演化的几何图形。研究驱动参数、膜的性质与所得光图形之间的关系。

研究线索：

驱动频率、振幅、膜张力、镜片位置、模态叠加与光程

07BUOYANCY · STABILITY

下沉漏斗

Sinking funnel

When a long-stemmed and sufficiently heavy funnel is released vertically into water with the stem pointing downward, it undergoes a series of damped vertical oscillations while gradually sinking. Investigate the motion and stability of the sinking funnel and the conditions under which the funnel tips over.

将一只长柄且足够重的漏斗以柄朝下的方向竖直释放到水中时，它会在逐渐下沉的同时经历一系列衰减的竖直振荡。研究下沉漏斗的运动与稳定性，以及漏斗发生倾覆的条件。

研究线索：

质量分布、排水体积、柄长、阻力、入水姿态与倾覆阈值

08FRICTION · DRIVEN MOTION

向上驱动圆盘

Upward-driven disc

A circular disc is suspended from the point where it is sandwiched between two wheels, making it free to oscillate as a pendulum. One of the wheels is driven by a motor and thus exerts an upward driving force on the disc. Investigate the motion of the disc.

一个圆盘悬挂在被两只轮子夹住的接触点上，因此可以像摆一样自由振荡。其中一只轮子由电机驱动，从而对圆盘施加向上的驱动力。研究圆盘的运动。

研究线索：

轮速、摩擦系数、夹持力、圆盘尺寸、质量分布与非线性稳定态

09IMAGING · APERTURE

摄影散景

Photographic bokeh

Bokeh is an effect observed in photographs that show objects at different distances from the camera and have both sharp and out-of-focus areas. If a slit is placed in front of the lens of the camera, the image may have unusual anisotropic distortions in out-of-focus areas, while in-focus areas remain undistorted and sharp. Investigate how the properties and sharpness of resulting images depend on relevant parameters.

散景是一种出现在摄影图像中的效果：画面包含距相机不同距离的物体，同时具有清晰区域与焦外区域。若在相机镜头前放置一道狭缝，图像的焦外区域可能出现异常的各向异性畸变，而合焦区域仍保持无畸变且清晰。研究所得图像的性质与锐度如何依赖于相关参数。

研究线索：

狭缝宽度与方向、焦距、光圈、物距、离焦量与点扩散函数

10VORTEX · FREE SURFACE

空气涡管

Air vortex

Place a small rod-shaped magnet on the bottom of a vessel filled with liquid and set it into rotation using a magnetic stirrer. Above a certain rotation speed, a thin air channel forms, extending from the liquid surface down to the rotating magnet itself. Investigate and explain this phenomenon.

将一枚小棒状磁铁放在盛有液体的容器底部，并用磁力搅拌器使其旋转。当转速超过某一数值时，会形成一条细长的空气通道，从液面一直延伸到旋转磁铁本身。研究并解释这一现象。

研究线索：

转速、液深、黏度、容器尺度、表面张力与核心稳定性

11ACOUSTICS · OPENING

隔音缺口

Sound isolation

The sound isolation of a room may become breached if the door is not shut properly, even if the gap is negligibly small. Investigate how the shape and size of a small opening affect the transmission of sound.

如果房门没有正确关紧，即使缝隙小到可以忽略，也可能破坏房间的隔音效果。研究微小开口的形状与尺寸如何影响声音的传播。

研究线索：

缝隙面积、长宽比、位置、声波频率、边缘效应与腔体共振

12STICK-SLIP · WRITING

虚线把戏

Dotted line trick

Under certain conditions, when a piece of chalk is dragged across a blackboard, the resulting trace is not a continuous line but a periodic sequence of dots, even though the motion is uninterrupted. This can happen with other writing materials and surfaces. Investigate the physical parameters that govern this phenomenon.

在特定条件下，当粉笔划过黑板时，即使运动从未中断，留下的痕迹也不是连续线，而是一列周期性圆点。其他书写材料与表面也可能出现这种现象。研究控制该现象的物理参数。

研究线索：

速度、压力、倾角、材料弹性、表面粗糙度与黏滑振荡

13FLOW · VORTEX SHEDDING

涡激摆

Vortex pendulum

A pendulum with a vertically oriented cylindrical bob is suspended above a channel of flowing water. When the bob is partially submerged, the flow may cause the pendulum to oscillate in a direction perpendicular to the flow. Explain this phenomenon and determine how it depends on relevant parameters.

一个摆锤为竖直圆柱体的摆悬挂在流水槽上方。当摆锤部分浸入水中时，水流可能使摆沿垂直于流向的方向振荡。解释这一现象，并确定它如何依赖于相关参数。

研究线索：

流速、浸没深度、圆柱直径、摆长、斯特劳哈尔数与同步区间

14NON-NEWTONIAN · PATTERN

非牛顿流体“蠕虫”

Non-Newtonian worms

If a dilatant non-Newtonian fluid (e.g. a suspension of water and cornflour) is vibrated vertically in a reservoir. When certain conditions are met, wormlike structures that rise from the surface may form. Explain and investigate this phenomenon.

若在容器中竖直振动剪切增稠型非牛顿流体（例如水与玉米粉的悬浊液），在满足特定条件时，表面可能形成向上升起的虫状结构。解释并研究这一现象。

研究线索：

加速度、频率、浓度、层深、屈服行为、表面张力与图样阈值

15THERMODYNAMICS · COOLING

冰镇饮料

Cold drink

A can or bottle of drink at room temperature can be cooled in a cooler containing ice. Adding salt and alcohol to the ice can increase the rate of cooling and affect the drink's final temperature. Investigate how the relevant parameters affect the lowest achievable temperature and the maximum cooling rate.

室温下的一罐或一瓶饮料可以在装有冰的冷却容器中降温。向冰中加入盐和酒精能够提高冷却速率，并影响饮料的最终温度。研究相关参数如何影响可达到的最低温度与最大冷却速率。

研究线索：

盐与酒精浓度、冰水比、搅拌、接触面积、相变与换热系数

16MAGNETISM · TRANSPORT

磁力旋转木马

Magnetic carousel

Several neodymium magnets are attached to a horizontal disc, with adjacent magnets oriented in opposite directions. A fixed non-magnetic plate is placed horizontally above the disc. When the disc rotates about its vertical axis and a steel ball is placed on the plate, the ball may move either in the same direction as, or opposite to, the rotation of the disc. Investigate this phenomenon and determine how the motion depends on relevant parameters.

若干钕磁铁固定在水平圆盘上，相邻磁铁的方向彼此相反。圆盘上方水平放置一块固定的非磁性平板。当圆盘绕竖直轴旋转并把钢球放在平板上时，钢球既可能沿圆盘转动方向运动，也可能反向运动。研究这一现象，并确定该运动如何依赖于相关参数。

研究线索：

转速、磁极排列、板厚、球尺寸、涡流、滚动摩擦与相位滞后

17RIGID BODY · CONTACT

落下的书封

Falling book cover

A book is placed horizontally, and the partially open cover is allowed to fall. Investigate the motion of the falling book cover.

将一本书水平放置，并让部分张开的封面自行落下。研究书封下落时的运动。

研究线索：

初始角、封面刚度、质量分布、书脊结构、空气阻力与碰撞过程
